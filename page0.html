Kyra Chhiu-Lim is a Digital Media Art student at San Jose State University exploring various mediums of digital media including video, web design, mixed media, and installation. To create her art, she utilizes tools such as adobe premiere, photoshop, atom, and illustrator. Kyra addresses subjects relating to identity, surveillance, and the 21st century's fast growing technology.
