State of Emergency

ABOUT

"State of Emergency" pokes at the fear that the fast growing technology and rise of AI robots have on contemporary society. The uncanny valley in aesthetics, is a hypothesized relationship between the degree of an object's resemblance to a human being and the emotional response to such an object. It explains why human-like robots freak us out. Inspired by this concept, Kyra creates a scenario where robots have gained the power to overthrow human control."
